Color Scheme

The color scheme for this website will be primary colors with the addition of green and the neutrals of white, gray, and black. This can be referred to as a square color scheme. There will be some additions of the gradient shown using all 4 main colors. The color palette diagram shows the color palette.


Color Application

  • The title and body copy will be black.
  • The navigation bar will be blue, green, and transparent.
  • The section headings will be black or blue.
  • The background color will be a gradient of the color palette or a picture.
  • The header will be part of the picture background or a lighter gradient color.
  • The footer will be part red with white type or a color gradient.
