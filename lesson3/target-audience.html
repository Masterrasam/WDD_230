Description of Target Audience

My target audience will be community members who are looking for businesses within the community, business owners that are looking for other professionals and information, and new community members looking for businesses within the area.

Scenarios

  • Is the new gym in the community a member of the chamber of commerce?
  • I want to open an ice cream shop and need advice from local business owners.
  • How many businesses are members of our local chamber of commerce?
  • I wonder if their is a health food store in the area?
  • I want some information about the banks in our area.

Persona #1 - christabelle

christabelle is a 32 year old owner of a clothing boutique. She has had a successful small business in her hometown, but is looking to move to move to a larger space in the center of Hollyhill. She wants to learn more about the area from the local businesses and see if there is a space that would be right for her clothing store. She wants advice and information about the area to see if her business will be successful if she relocates to the area.

Persona #2 - lycon

lycon is a 29 year old personal trainer. He has been working at a gym for 3 years and is planning to move to Hollyhill within the next 6 months. He needs to network with businesses and gyms in the Hollyhill area to find another gym to train at. He also plans to open a gym of his own in the next 5 years and would like help and advice from business owners that know the community. He wants financial information and help with a business plan to help his business venture succeed.
